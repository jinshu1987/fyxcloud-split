AI is moving faster than security teams can track it. Models, agents, datasets, vector databases, and prompt pipelines are being deployed across multiple clouds—often without oversight. Traditional CSPM tools see infrastructure, but they cannot see the intelligence layer running on top of it.

Fyx Cloud AI introduces a new category: AI Security Posture Management (AI-SPM). It discovers every AI asset, maps the entire AI supply chain, and detects toxic security paths before they become breaches. With over 100 precision detection rules, Fyx continuously secures your AI environment—from training data to runtime inference.

Product Overview
What Fyx Cloud AI Does

Fyx Cloud AI is a cloud-native AI Security Posture Management platform designed to secure the complete lifecycle of AI systems.

It provides continuous visibility into:

AI models and endpoints

AI agents and prompt flows

vector databases and knowledge bases

training datasets and feature stores

AI identities and permissions

runtime AI interactions

Fyx connects these components into a unified risk graph, enabling organizations to detect and prioritize security risks that traditional security tools miss.

The platform works across AWS, Azure, and Google Cloud, integrating directly with cloud APIs to inventory AI resources, analyze configurations, and enforce security policies.

2. Shadow AI Detection

Fyx identifies unsanctioned AI systems operating outside approved environments.

Key capabilities

Shadow SaaS AI Detection
Detects outbound traffic to unauthorized AI services including OpenAI, Anthropic, Mistral, and other AI APIs.

Rogue GPU Compute Detection
Identifies GPU-enabled instances used for unapproved AI workloads.

Unmanaged Model Deployments
Flags model endpoints that are not registered in the official AI inventory.

Shadow Data Pipelines
Detects ETL pipelines moving production data into AI environments without approval.

Local LLM Detection
Identifies self-hosted AI systems such as Ollama or vLLM deployed on cloud compute.

Unauthorized OAuth AI Apps
Detects AI SaaS applications integrated via SSO without approval.

3. Toxic Path Risk Analysis

Fyx uses a graph-based risk engine to detect multi-step attack paths.

Key capabilities

Identity-Data-Model Correlation
Links IAM roles, data sources, and model endpoints to identify real attack paths.

Critical Risk Path Detection
Identifies combinations such as:

Public data bucket → Overprivileged IAM role → AI model endpoint

Prioritized Remediation
Focuses on the few security paths that could lead to real breaches.

Attack Path Visualization
Provides visual mapping of AI infrastructure relationships.
